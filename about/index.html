About the DOTA2 Team Builder

I've been playing DOTA2 for about 300 hours. Normally, this would feel like a lot, but as deep as the game is I still feel like I have a lot to learn. I wanted to build this tool to help both myself as well as others and give them a nice shift+tab guide that helped them pick a well-balanced team.

The problem is I'm still not sure what makes up a good team. There are a number of rules that are used on this site to generate advice, but they're almost guaranteed to be inadequate or downright inaccurate. That's where you (hopefully) come in.

How you can help

I need advanced and expert players to help me refine the rules of this team builder. No coding knowledge is needed (though if you are a frontend person and want to make it look prettier, please let me know).

I primarily need people who can 1) help refine the individual hero role rankings; and 2) help refine the algorithms that determine what advice to give.

If you are interested, please send me an email at [ellisd23] at [gmail] [dot] [com]. If you have a dotabuff account or some other credentials to show that you are experienced, please send that along as well.

I hope to ultimately improve the quality of pub play by providing a resource that helps players choose their characters more wisely.
